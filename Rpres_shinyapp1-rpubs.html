A shiny app to help you keep hydrated calculating how much water you need to drink each day.

Summary

I am pleased to introduce you to a new shiny app that calculated how much water you need to drink each day based on:

  • weight
  • activity level

You can choose between metric or US units.

How it works

It is a very simple function that takes your weight (lbs) and multiply it by 2/3 getting the water intake (in ounces) that is necessary without activity. Then for every 30 minutes of activity, it adds 12 ounces.

For example, if you weight 200 pounds (lbs) and your activity level is 90 minutes/day.

weight <- 200
activitylevel <- 90

wl <- round((weight * 2 / 3) + ((activitylevel / 30) * 12))

wl 

[1] 169

The result is 169 ounces that day.
